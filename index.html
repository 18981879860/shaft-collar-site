Frequently Asked Questions

Can JINGBANG manufacture custom non-standard shaft collars?
Yes. We manufacture shaft collars with custom bore sizes, lengths, materials, and special features like keyways, set screw positions, or flanges. Send your 2D/3D CAD for a DFM review.
What tolerances can you achieve?
Our standard bore tolerance is +0.03mm to +0.08mm for slip-fit applications. Tighter tolerances down to +0.01mm are available upon request.
Do you provide raw material certificates?
Yes. We provide mill test reports (MTR) for stainless steel, aluminum, and alloy steel upon request.
What is your typical lead time for custom shaft collars?
7-15 working days for quantities under 500 pieces. Rapid prototyping available in 5 days.
Can I get a sample before mass production?
Yes. Sample orders are welcomed. Sample cost is refundable with bulk order.
